RAIN WATER HARVESTING

Rainwater harvesting (RWH) is the practice of collecting and storing rainwater for later use, rather than letting it run off. This method involves capturing rainwater from surfaces like rooftops and directing it into storage systems such as tanks, cisterns, or even underground reservoirs

Advantages of Rain water harvesting

  1. 1. Helps Reducing Watre bills
  2. 2. Decreases the demand for water
  3. 3. Reduces the improted water
